CBR650RR
The Honda CBR600RR is a high-performance sports motorcycle, equipped with a 599 cm³ inline 4-cylinder engine that generates around 118 hp at 13,500 rpm. Its design is focused on offering excellent maneuverability and stability, both on circuits and on roads, with a double-spar aluminum chassis, inverted fork front suspension and adjustable Pro-Link rear suspension. The CBR600RR features 310mm disc brakes with ABS (on some S models) and optimized aerodynamics with full fairing.
