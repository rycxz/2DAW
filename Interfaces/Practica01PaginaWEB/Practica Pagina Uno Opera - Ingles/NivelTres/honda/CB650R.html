CB650R
The Honda CB650R is a 649 cm³ naked sports motorcycle that shares the same inline 4-cylinder engine base with the CBR650R, delivering around 94 hp at 12,000 rpm. Its minimalist and modern design makes it ideal for those looking for a stylish and high-performance motorcycle without the full fairing of a sports car. It is equipped with an aluminum double-spar chassis, inverted fork front suspension and an adjustable rear monoshock.
