Honda CBR Fireblade

The Honda CBR1000RR Fireblade is a high-end sports motorcycle designed to deliver exceptional performance both on the road and on the track. Its 999 cm³, inline 4-cylinder engine generates around 189 hp at 13,000 rpm, making it one of the most powerful bikes in its class.

Equipped with an aluminum chassis, USD front fork suspension, and adjustable Pro-Link rear suspension, it offers precise handling and stability at high speeds.
