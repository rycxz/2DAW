Honda CB650R

The Honda CB650R is a 649 cc naked sport motorcycle that shares the same 4-cylinder inline engine base with the CBR650R, delivering around 94 hp at 12,000 rpm.

Its minimalist and modern design makes it ideal for those looking for a stylish and high-performance bike without the full fairing of a sportbike. It is equipped with a twin-spar aluminum frame, inverted front fork suspension, and an adjustable rear monoshock.
