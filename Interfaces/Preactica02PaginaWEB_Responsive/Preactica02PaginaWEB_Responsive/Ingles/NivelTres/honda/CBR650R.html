Honda CBR650R

For the purest sports performance, the DOHC inline four-cylinder engine with 16 valves produces 70 kW/94 HP at peak (reaching 12,000 rpm at maximum speed) with 63 Nm of torque for smooth corner exits.

There is a 35 kW conversion for the A2 license, which is ready to run at full power once the A license is obtained. Additionally, as power needs control, an assist and slipper clutch manages the rear wheel during hard braking and downshifting to prevent it from locking.
