Z 900 A2 2024

The Kawasaki Z900 A2 2024 is a naked motorcycle that combines power, dynamic design, and accessibility for A2 license holders. With its 948 cc engine tuned to meet A2 license requirements, it offers controlled and linear power delivery that makes riding agile and fun without compromising safety.

It stands out for its aggressive and minimalist design in line with Kawasaki's "Sugomi" aesthetics, giving it a muscular and modern appearance. Its lightweight and balanced chassis, along with quality suspension and high-performance brakes, ensure precise handling and great stability on any type of terrain.
