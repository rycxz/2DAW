Suzuki GSX-8R

The Suzuki GSX-8R combines style, performance, and fun. Engine of 776 cm³, 4-stroke, 2 cylinders, DOHC, liquid cooling. 6-speed transmission, fuel consumption of 4.2L/100km, power of 82 HP at 8500 rpm, maximum torque of 75 Nm at 6500 rpm.

Front suspension of inverted telescopic fork and rear suspension of mono-shock. Front brakes with dual disc and rear brake with single disc. Front tire 120/70ZR17 and rear tire 180/55ZR17. Fuel tank capacity of 16 liters and weight of 202 kg.
